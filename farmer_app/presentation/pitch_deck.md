# Smart Farming: The "Hackathon Winner" Pitch Deck

---

## Slide 1: Title

**Title**: Smart Farming Application for Irrigation Management and Crop Health Monitoring
**Subtitle**: Empowering Farmers with Data-Driven Decisions
**Visual**: A professional and intuitive dashboard showing irrigation alerts and crop health stats.

---

## Slide 2: The Problem

**Headline**: Challenges in Rural Irrigation & Crop Health.

- **Pain Point**: Difficulty in deciding the right time for irrigation and identifying crop issues.
- **Connectivity**: Limited access to internet in rural areas hinders modern tool adoption.
- **Economic Loss**: Improper irrigation and delayed disease detection lead to reduced yield and water wastage.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution

**Headline**: A Hybrid Online-Offline Smart Farming Application.

1.  **Irrigation**: Smart recommendations based on soil moisture and climate data.
2.  **Health**: Early disease prediction via image processing and analysis.
3.  **Sync**: Offline data storage with automatic online synchronization.

---

## Slide 4: "Winner" Tech Stack

**Headline**: Built for Scale & Speed.

- **Frontend**: Flutter + Riverpod (Glassmorphic Design).
- **AI/ML**: Hybrid Execution (Edge TFLite + Cloud FastAPI Verification).
- **Offline**: Hive NoSQL + Background Sync Queue.
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python 3.14) + Firebase Firestore.

---

## Slide 5: Impact

**Headline**: Driving Sustainable Agriculture.

- **Sustainability**: Optimizes water usage and reduces wastage through precise irrigation.
- **Efficiency**: Reduces crop loss by detecting diseases at an early stage.
- **Empowerment**: Provides farmers with accessible and intelligent decision-making tools.
